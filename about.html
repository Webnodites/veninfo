Our Team

Meet our team

→ Who will be handling your project? This is one of the questions we get from most of our clients. The good news is that we have a dedicated team for the smooth running of your project. We will cover all aspects of your business and have everything delivered as promised.

Project Leader

A project manager will be assigned to a couple of projects in their area of expertise and have every project running in the said time. They will have the team leaders of each project reporting to them on a set schedule for easier management. The project manager ensures all problems encountered are mitigated in the best possible way.

Team Leader

When you give us a task, we will have a team lead expert who ensures that the team meets all goals. The leader ensures that you will have everyone working towards the set targets and are being achieved promptly.

Consultants

We have a team of consultants experienced in the logistics field. You will get tried and tested recommendations for your business with their expertise. They will help you identify loopholes in your production line, offer solutions and give cases where the recommended solutions have been implemented and worked.

IT Experts

The IT experts will be responsible for the solutions tailored for you and ensure they have the correct code and are working as expected. They will ensure that you get all functionalities depending on your needs. They will also be involved in training your personnel in some events or help the designated trainers to ensure that the systems are used as required and effectively.

Veninfos harnesses the power of SAP to present you with a wealth of services. Our business consultancy solutions include management, intelligence, resource planning and consolidation. Customised development services, human capital management, and support services are some of our specialities. Our SAP system support solutions include assistance with rollout, migration, upgrades, offshore assistance, hosting, and more.
